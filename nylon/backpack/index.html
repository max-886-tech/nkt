Materials & Manufacturing

We manufacture backpacks in a wide range of materials including nylon, polyester, canvas, PP and genuine or vegan leather. Nylon and polyester remain the most popular choices thanks to their strength, light weight and colour options.

100% waterproof construction is possible with special fabrics, heat-sealed seams and coated zippers. For most corporate and institute orders we recommend “water-resistant” backpacks that balance protection, looks and cost.

Customisation & Branding

Every backpack project can be customised – fabric colours, contrast panels, zipper pullers, inner organisers and back padding can all be tuned as per your brief. We can add your branding using embroidery, printing, patches or metal badges depending on the design.

Our team works closely with your marketing or purchase team to finalise branding placement so your logo is clearly visible without compromising the overall aesthetics of the bag.

Who We Work With

Nekton India supplies backpacks to institutes, coaching centres, corporates, exporters, e-commerce brands and merchandising companies. Whether you need 100 bags or 10,000+ units, we can plan production and logistics accordingly.

We also manufacture related products like laptop sleeves, duffle bags, trolleys, courier bags and leather accessories – making it easier to source your entire range from a single vendor.
